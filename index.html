About Synergy/ Empowering Innovation

Synergy is North India's most prodigious and largest technical festival organized under the aegis of Software Developers DTU.

In the month of February, hundreds of competitive coders will code-shed each other, hundreds of designers and developers will code, develop and deploy innovative products and top professionals from industry will weave the way to success of budding developers.

Witness the grandeur of Synergy'17, it's the year for new experiences!

FAQ / Judge a man by his questions rather than his answers

  • Who runs the Synergy'17?
  • How to register for events under Synergy'17?
  • Can we register as a team for Finquest or Code War?
  • What type of events are hackDTU and Quedar?
  • Is there any screening process for all these events?
  • How will be the events judged?

It is run by Software Developers, Delhi Technological University, a student organization pledged to enrich the development culture in the country.

Registration process ends on February 1, 2017. Register earlier to avoid last minute hustle. Applying earlier also means that we'll have more time to assess your application.

No, you have to register individually for these events.

Finquest is an online event to be held on 5-6 February 2017. After registration, you'll get a confirmation mail with login credentials, use these credentials to login on finquest.sddtu.org and start playing the virtual stock market challenge.

Code War is Open Arena Competitive Coding Knockout Tournament, you have to be a lone warrior in this event. Batches, each with few competitive coders (depending on the number of participants) will be formed and the winner from each batch will proceed to next level. This procedure is repeated at each level till we have the last man standing.

hackDTU is a team event where a maximum of 4 hackers can form a single team. But, you are free to register individually. Also, if you need a team, we will pair you with other individuals.

Quedar is a free to attend conference, after registration, you'll get a confirmation mail with attached event pass. You have to bring printed copy of the pass with you to the event.

No such screening, but the selection of registration form in hackDTU, Code War and Quedar depends on the number of applications received.

You can browse to respective events' pages to find rule books. All rules pertaining to a specific event are listed in the rule book. There is no rule book for Quedar (Conference).
